HOW TO PLAY

1. Choose map → answer 3 immunology questions before each wave.
2. Click a tower then click the grid to place it off the path.
3. Correct answers earn bonus antibodies (gold) per wave.
4. Every 10 levels a BOSS appears — massive HP with special traits!
5. Earn Skill Points each wave. Open Skills to permanently upgrade towers.
6. Use 💉💊🧪 power-ups at the right moment. Keys 1/2/3.
